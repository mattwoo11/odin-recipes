Skillet Fries

Description

Pan-fry potatoes in a skillet.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 3 large potatoes, sliced lengthwise into 1/2-inch circles and cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • 1/4 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir potato strips in hot oil mixture until browned on all sides, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Transfer fries to a paper towel-lined plate; season with sea salt and cracked black pepper.
