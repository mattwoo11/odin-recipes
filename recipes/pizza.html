Pizza

Description

Pizza is a Italian dish that is made out of flat dough in a circle size that includes tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni/ other toppings
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475F.
  2. Make the dough flat.
  3. Use a brush to cover the dough with olive oil and rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce on the dough, sprinkle the cheese around the dough, and place the pepperoni or any other toppings on the dough.
  5. Put the pizza in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes until the crust is brown and the cheese is golden.
  6. You can add more cheese if you think the pizza needs it.
