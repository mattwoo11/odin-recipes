Kettle Corn

Description

Kettle corn is a type of popcorn that is sweet compare to regular popcorn.

Ingredients

  • Popcorn kernels
  • Oil
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook the oil in a large pot in medium heat.
  2. Stir in the popcorn kernels and sugar in the pot.
  3. Put a cover on the pot and shake it until poping has slowed down once every 2 to 3 seconds, remove the pot from the heat and keep shaking until it stops poping.
  4. Pour the popcorn in a large bowl and let it cool down. Stir it occasionally to break into large clumps.
